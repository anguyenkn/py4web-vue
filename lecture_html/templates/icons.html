Icons

We use Font Awesome 4.7.0 icons. I know that Font Awesome 5.0 is more fashionable, but the extra icons are not free, and it actually makes debugging harder. In software, not everything that is newer is necessarily better!

To include an icon, you include a tag; look at the source code and you will figure it out! Looking at the examples is also very useful.
